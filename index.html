Frequently Asked Questions

How is Ascend different from booking a local therapist?

We offer integrated strategic and coordination services that go beyond weekly counselling. While therapy focuses on emotional processing, Ascend provides the structure for crisis planning, case management, and external provider alignment, which is essential for complex, high-discretion situations.

How is confidentiality and privacy guaranteed?

As a Swiss-based entity, we are bound by the Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP), one of the world's strictest. All communication is secure, and client case details are handled with absolute clinical discretion, ensuring the highest level of privacy protection globally.

What is the difference between Case Management and 24/7 Coaching?

Case Management is strategic, logistical, and clinical coordination (managing the 'systems' around the client). 24/7 Coaching is direct, continuous, on-the-ground support and accountability for the client in their daily life. Both are distinct, but often work together for comprehensive care.

Are your services suitable for complex, high-stakes family situations?

Yes. We specialize in navigating the unique privacy demands and family systems of UHNW and high-profile clientele. Our 25+ years of experience is dedicated to stabilizing complex situations and creating system-wide solutions for families in crisis.
