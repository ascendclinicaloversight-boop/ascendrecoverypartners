Biography & Professional Commitment

Dean's foundation is built on a quarter-century of practice underpinned by rigorous clinical education, including a Higher National Diploma in Counselling (Weston College), a Post-Graduate Certificate in Counselling Supervision (Filton College), and a Level 3 Certificate in Supervisory Management & Leadership (2010).

His career began with direct client care as a Counsellor/Psychotherapist at Chandos House Treatment Centre, providing individual and group therapy for behaviour change.

For over a decade (2003–2013), Dean dedicated himself to leadership in the charity sector at the Addiction Recovery Agency. As a Treatment Service Manager, he was responsible for developing teams, improving client outcomes, and mastering the complex skills of service design and organizational management.

A Specialist in High-Stakes Care:

From 2013 to 2025 at The Kusnacht Practice, Dean applied his expertise to the field of ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) private care. His roles included Live-in Counsellor, Head of Clinical Operations, and Relapse Prevention Specialist. This twelve-year specialization provides Ascend Recovery Partners with unmatched insight into the discretion, ethical complexity, and intense demands placed on high-profile clients, their families, and the professionals who support them.

Dean is committed to bringing this blend of organizational insight, deep clinical ability (Integrated Humanistic Counselling), and executive management to every service offered, whether it is direct family guidance or specialist supervision.
